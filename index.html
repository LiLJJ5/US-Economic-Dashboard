About This Dashboard

This dashboard provides real-time economic metrics for the United States using free public APIs. The data is automatically refreshed daily to provide the most current information available.

All data is sourced from official government agencies and reputable financial data providers through their public APIs, including:

  • Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)
  • U.S. Department of the Treasury
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA)
  • U.S. Census Bureau

Key metrics displayed include:

  • Federal Debt to GDP Ratio - Shows the U.S. federal debt as a percentage of GDP, indicating the country's debt burden relative to its economic output
  • Unemployment Rate - The percentage of the labor force that is jobless and actively seeking employment
  • Inflation Rate - The rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services is rising
  • S&P 500 Index - A stock market index tracking the performance of 500 large companies listed on U.S. exchanges
  • 10-Year Treasury Yield - The return on investment for the U.S. government's 10-year debt obligation

This dashboard is built using free and open-source technologies:

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Bootstrap for responsive design
  • Chart.js for data visualization
  • Free public APIs for data retrieval
